## Overall Impression

The system itself is disciplined and the copy honors the "спокойный, административный" brief the whole way through. The gap is between the *system's* restraint and the *homepage's structure*: the page that most needs to feel calming to a stressed, jargon-unfamiliar reader is currently the densest, least-sequenced page on the site — four bad-news status cards front-loaded with no reassurance until a footer sentence almost nobody will read, and jargon introduced before it's ever explained. The single biggest opportunity is homepage sequencing, not new visual work.

## Priority Issues

**[P1] Homepage primary CTA is buried behind ~1.5 mobile screens of bad news.**
Why it matters: audience reads "с телефона, между делом" (PRODUCT.md). On ≤640px, the reader scrolls h1 → lede → 4 full-width stacked status cards before reaching the CTA — the highest-abandonment stretch on the site's most-visited page.
Fix: move `.cta-row` immediately after `.lede`, before `.anomaly-strip`; let the cards serve as scannable reference below the fold, not a gate in front of the action.
Suggested command: `/impeccable layout`

**[P1] `.compare-table` loses the row's topic label on mobile horizontal scroll.**
Why it matters: `min-width:640px` inside an `overflow-x:auto` wrapper forces horizontal scroll on phones; the first column isn't sticky, so scrolling to read Статус pushes Тема off-screen — a textbook Memory Bridge cognitive-load violation on the site's fastest claim-verification artifact.
Fix: `position: sticky; left: 0; background: var(--paper-alt)` on `.compare-table td:first-child`/`th:first-child`.
Suggested command: `/impeccable harden`

**[P2] No skip-to-content link across all 15 pages.**
Why it matters: 7-item nav precedes `<main>` everywhere with no bypass — a real, cumulative cost for keyboard/screen-reader users across a 15-page site.
Fix: visually-hidden-until-focus skip link as the first focusable element in `<body>`.
Suggested command: `/impeccable harden`

**[P2] Peak-end structure works against the site's own calm-tone commitment.**
Why it matters: the one sentence that would prime trust before four alarming cards ("обвинительные формулировки не используются...") lives only in small grey footer text — least likely to be read by someone who scans and leaves.
Fix: surface a short version near the lede, or give the hero's "действие" pointer item (currently last) more weight as an explicit close.
Suggested command: `/impeccable clarify`

**[P2] Type scale under-documented, not over-used.** (Assessment B)
15 inline font-sizes on `dovod-290-po-godam.html` (`0.82rem`/`0.78rem`, consistent repetition) fall outside DESIGN.md's 3-role ramp. The values themselves are consistent — DESIGN.md's type section needs the missing steps added, not a CSS cleanup.
Suggested command: `/impeccable document` (regenerate DESIGN.md's type section) or `/impeccable typeset`

**[P3] `.anomaly-strip`/`.pointer-grid` are plain `<div>`s, not semantic lists.**
Why it matters: both are parallel comparable-item sets (4 and 3 items); without `<ul>/<li>` or `role="list"`, screen readers lose the "list of N" framing.
Fix: wrap items in `<ul>/<li>` or add `role="list"`/`role="listitem"`.
Suggested command: `/impeccable harden`

**[P3] Flat type hierarchy on one data page.** (Assessment B)
`dovod-290-po-godam.html` line 103: three sizes (12.5/13.1/15.2px) at 1.2:1 ratio, below the 1.25 guideline. Isolated, low-impact — that page is deliberately utilitarian.
Suggested command: `/impeccable typeset`
